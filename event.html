When

Doors will open at 5:30 p.m. The wedding will start at 6:00.

Attire & format

The reception will be “cocktail-style”—no fixed seating, with passed hors d’oeuvres and food stations. Comfortable footwear is strongly recommended!

Attire is “dressy casual.” We’re interpreting that as “Dress up a little, but be comfortable.” Some rough guidelines:

  • Men: Slacks (or nice jeans) and a nice button-down shirt are good. Jacket and tie are both optional but certainly appropriate. You should feel free to wear a suit if you feel like it!
  • Women: A nice shirt, dress pants (or nice jeans), or skirt, or a cocktail dress.
